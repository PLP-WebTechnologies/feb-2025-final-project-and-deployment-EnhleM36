Classic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil.
  3. Mix pasta and seasonings, then serve hot.
