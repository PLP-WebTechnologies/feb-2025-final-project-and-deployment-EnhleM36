Street Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • Small corn tortillas
  • Onion, cilantro, lime
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Cook beef with spices.
  2. Warm tortillas and fill with meat.
  3. Top with chopped onion, cilantro, and lime juice.
